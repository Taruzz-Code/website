Hamilton Lake

Join a peaceful virtual walk around Hamilton Lake also known as (Rotoroa), a unique nature spot in Waikato, New Zealand. It was Formed long time ago, it is important to Māori community, and now it’s a relaxing public park. Our video guides you along the 3.8km flat path, by showing you where people walk, cycle, and enjoy water activities like: -kayaking, paddle boating, and dragon boating, all with lovely green surroundings.

The reason I choose Hamilton Lake for this video is that it’s one of the most viewing placing in Hamilton Waikato. Its calm, pretty views and gentle activity make it a great place to unwind, especially for those unable to visit. This video aims to share the calming, beautiful feeling of this Waikato spot, offering you a peaceful break.

Waikato river

Take a virtual journey along the beautiful Waikato River, New Zealand's longest river! For centuries, this river has been a special place, important to Māori for food, travel, and as a treasure. It has seen many changes, from ancient times when the land was formed, to the arrival of people and the growth of towns along its banks.

This video was chosen to bring the calming sights and sounds of this historic waterway to you, offering a peaceful escape into nature, especially for those who find it hard to get outdoors. Imagine yourself walking or gently cruising along the Waikato. People come here to enjoy many things, like walking and cycling on paths like Te Awa River Ride, kayaking on the water, or simply relaxing and enjoying the views. Our virtual tour lets you experience this special place from wherever you are. It's a chance to see the flowing water, the green riverbanks, and feel a connection to nature, all through your screen, bringing the New Zealand outdoors to everyone.
